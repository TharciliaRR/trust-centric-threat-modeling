# Research Notes

This section contains exploratory research papers related to the Trust-Centric Threat Modeling Framework (TCTM).

The purpose of these notes is to investigate emerging concepts at the intersection of:

* Cybersecurity
* AI Governance
* Digital Trust
* Autonomous Systems
* Trustworthy AI

## Research Areas

### Trust Engineering

Can trust be engineered and measured?

### Decision Security

Can decisions become attack surfaces?

### Trust Observability

Can trust be monitored continuously?

### Agent Governance

How should organizations govern autonomous agents?

### AI Assurance

How can organizations validate trustworthy autonomy?

These research notes serve as the foundation for future publications, white papers, conference papers, and academic research initiatives.
