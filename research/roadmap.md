# TCTM Research Roadmap

## Vision

The Trust-Centric Threat Modeling Framework (TCTM) aims to establish trust as a measurable, governable, and engineering-oriented capability for autonomous AI systems.

The long-term objective is to develop a comprehensive body of research supporting trustworthy autonomy across cybersecurity, governance, risk management, and decision intelligence domains.

# Ultimate Vision

The long-term vision of TCTM is to contribute toward a future in which organizations can confidently deploy autonomous AI systems supported by measurable trust, effective governance, resilient security, and evidence-based assurance.

In the era of autonomous AI, trust will become a strategic capability.

The objective of TCTM is to help organizations understand, measure, govern, and preserve that trust.
