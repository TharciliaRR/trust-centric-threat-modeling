# Trust-Centric Threat Modeling for Autonomous AI Systems

## Abstract

Autonomous AI systems introduce risks that extend beyond traditional cybersecurity concerns.

This paper proposes the Trust-Centric Threat Modeling Framework (TCTM), a methodology designed to evaluate trust, governance, decision integrity, and operational resilience in autonomous AI environments.

The framework introduces five Trust Domains, a trust-oriented threat taxonomy, a Trust Risk Matrix, and a Trust Maturity Model.

The objective is to establish trust as a measurable and governable organizational capability.
