# Research Areas

The TCTM research agenda explores the intersection of cybersecurity, AI governance, and digital trust.

---

# Trust Engineering

Engineering trust as an organizational capability.

---

# Decision Security

Protecting autonomous decisions against manipulation and degradation.

---

# Trust Observability

Monitoring trustworthiness through measurable indicators.

---

# Agent Governance

Establishing governance structures for autonomous AI agents.

---

# AI Assurance

Providing evidence that trust in autonomous systems is justified.

---

# Long-Term Goal

Establish trust as a measurable, governable, and operational capability for autonomous enterprises.
